- Keep billing unchanged when users skip a month.
- Rework referral system to reusable affiliate codes.
- Add affiliate dashboard with sharing, stats, and activity feed.

## Sprint B: Conversion + Trust
- Stripe customer portal deep-link for payment method management.
- Checkout success state with clear "plan active" confirmation.

- Pickup reminder cadence (T-3 days, T-1 day, day-of).

## Sprint C: Driver Operations
- Driver portal for route check-in, stop notes, and completion scans.
- Proof-of-pickup photo support on stops.
- Route status timeline in admin.
- Stop-level exception reasons (no bag, no access, reschedule).

## Sprint D: Growth + Neighborhood Expansion
- Ambassador landing pages for apartments/HOAs with tracked attribution.
- Zone heatmap for demand density and route viability.
- Partner organization management model (post-employee phase).
- Referral leaderboard and monthly bonus campaigns.

## Sprint E: Reliability + Scale
- Background jobs for notification sends and retries.
- Audit trail views in admin for key operational actions.
- Alerting for failed payments, missed pickups, and route gaps.
- KPI dashboard trend charts (subscribers, pickups, credits, churn).
